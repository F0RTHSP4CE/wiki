## Roles

### Guest
Every person visiting the hackspace is a guest, except for residents and members.
Guests may visit hackspace during events or accompanied by a resident.

Guests can use shared resources if it's provided on the event. In other cases, they should ask a resident or a member. 

### Member
Members can access hackspace anytime and use shared resources for their projects. They pay member fee. 

### Resident
Residents are active participants of the hackspace. Residents pay monthly fee for rent, utilities and development. Also, residents take part in decision making process. They also have access to the Resident room.
