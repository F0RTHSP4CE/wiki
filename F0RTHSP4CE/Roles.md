### Guest
Every person visiting hackspace is a guest, except for residents.
Guests may visit hackspace during events or accompanied by a resident.

Guests can use shared resources if it's provided on the event. In other cases, it should be agreed with resident.

### Resident
Residents are active participants of the hackspace. Residents pay monthly fee for rent, utilities and development. Also, residents take part in decision making process. They can access hackspace anytime and use shared resources for their projects.
